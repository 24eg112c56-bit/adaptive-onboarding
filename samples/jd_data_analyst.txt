JOB TITLE: Data Analyst

COMPANY: FinanceFlow Corp
LOCATION: Hybrid — Bangalore, India
EXPERIENCE: 2+ years

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are hiring a Data Analyst to help us make data-driven decisions.
You will work closely with business teams to analyze data, build dashboards,
and provide actionable insights.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Analyze large datasets to identify trends and patterns
- Build dashboards and reports using Power BI or Tableau
- Write complex SQL queries to extract and transform data
- Work with Python for data cleaning and automation
- Present findings to non-technical stakeholders
- Collaborate with engineering teams on data pipelines

REQUIRED SKILLS (Must-Have)
- SQL: Advanced — complex joins, window functions, stored procedures
- Python: Intermediate — pandas, numpy, data manipulation
- Data Analysis: Advanced — statistical analysis, trend identification
- Excel: Advanced — pivot tables, VLOOKUP, data visualization
- Communication: Strong — ability to present to business stakeholders
- Project Management: Basic — task tracking, meeting deadlines

NICE-TO-HAVE
- Machine Learning: basic predictive modeling
- Leadership: ability to guide junior analysts
- AWS: basic cloud data services

COMPENSATION
- CTC: 8-14 LPA
- Annual bonus
- Flexible work hours
